# CAP Method - Questionnaire Catalog

This catalog is the first source-of-truth layer for the CAP Method questionnaire suite.

The current Apps Script generator remains the operational implementation. This catalog prepares the next step: externalized questionnaire definitions that can later generate Google Forms, PDF versions, Blazor forms, and Microsoft Forms exports.

## Forms

| Code | Title | Moment | Main output |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| FORM-001 | Diagnostic initial du beneficiaire | Before session 1 | Initial profile and consultant preparation notes |
| FORM-002 | Valeurs professionnelles | After values work | Top values and alignment conditions |
| FORM-003 | Competences et realisations | During career analysis | Skills inventory and development needs |
| FORM-004 | Personnalite et mode de fonctionnement | During personal analysis | Working style and operating preferences |
| FORM-005 | Interets professionnels | Before or during exploration | Professional interests to explore |
| FORM-006 | Analyse des pistes professionnelles | After project options emerge | Feasibility and attractiveness analysis |
| FORM-007 | Plan d'action professionnel | End of journey | Operational action plan |
| FORM-008 | Evaluation intermediaire | Mid-journey | Adjustment points for the rest of the journey |
| FORM-009 | Evaluation finale du bilan | End of journey | Quality evaluation and improvement input |
| FORM-010 | Suivi a 6 mois | Six months after completion | Long-term impact indicators |

## FORM-001 - Diagnostic initial du beneficiaire

Goal: prepare the first interview and personalize the support journey.

Sections:

1. Bienvenue
2. Vos informations
3. Votre situation professionnelle
4. Satisfaction professionnelle
5. Raisons de la demarche
6. Attentes
7. Competences et reussites
8. Difficultes et freins
9. Projection

Expected output: initial beneficiary profile and consultant preparation notes.

## FORM-002 - Valeurs professionnelles

Goal: identify priority values and gaps with the current situation.

Sections:

1. Selection des valeurs
2. Priorisation
3. Projection

Expected output: top professional values and alignment conditions.

## FORM-003 - Competences et realisations

Goal: list technical skills, behavioral skills, achievements, and development needs.

Sections:

1. Competences techniques
2. Competences comportementales
3. Realisations
4. Developpement

Expected output: skills inventory and skills to develop.

## FORM-004 - Personnalite et mode de fonctionnement

Goal: understand work style, decision-making style, communication preferences, and stress management.

Sections:

1. Mode de travail
2. Decision et organisation
3. Communication et stress

Expected output: professional operating profile.

## FORM-005 - Interets professionnels

Goal: identify attractive activities, missions, sectors, and working environments.

Sections:

1. Activites preferees
2. Domaines et environnements
3. Motivation

Expected output: professional interests and exploration priorities.

## FORM-006 - Analyse des pistes professionnelles

Goal: evaluate attractiveness, feasibility, skills gaps, risks, and decision status for a professional option.

Sections:

1. Piste principale
2. Ecarts et besoins
3. Risques et decision

Expected output: retain, reject, or deepen a professional option.

## FORM-007 - Plan d'action professionnel

Goal: formalize objectives, actions, resources, deadlines, and success indicators.

Sections:

1. Objectif principal
2. Actions a realiser
3. Ressources et suivi

Expected output: operational action plan.

## FORM-008 - Evaluation intermediaire

Goal: measure progress and adjust the support journey.

Sections:

1. Satisfaction a mi-parcours
2. Difficultes et ajustements

Expected output: adjustment points for the next sessions.

## FORM-009 - Evaluation finale du bilan

Goal: measure final satisfaction, goal achievement, and tool usefulness.

Sections:

1. Satisfaction globale
2. Resultats obtenus
3. Amelioration continue

Expected output: final quality evaluation and continuous improvement input.

## FORM-010 - Suivi a 6 mois

Goal: measure the long-term impact of the skills assessment.

Sections:

1. Situation actuelle
2. Mise en oeuvre du projet
3. Besoins complementaires

Expected output: impact indicators and optional follow-up needs.
